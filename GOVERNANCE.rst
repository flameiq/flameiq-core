==========
Governance
==========

FlameIQ uses a **merit-based maintainer model**. Decisions are made by
people who have demonstrated sustained, high-quality contributions.

Maintainer responsibilities
============================

* Review and merge pull requests within 5 business days
* Maintain CI health
* Manage releases and PyPI publications
* Enforce the Code of Conduct

Decision making
================

Minor changes (bug fixes, docs, non-algorithm features)
    Maintainer approval, simple majority. No RFC required.

Major changes (algorithms, schema, architecture, CLI contracts)
    RFC required. Two-thirds maintainer vote after a 7-day comment period.
